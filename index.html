ABOUT CHEBAO

Quanzhou Chebao Automotive Products Co., Ltd. is located in Quanzhou, the cultural capital of East Asia, the starting point of the Maritime Silk Road, and the economic center of Fujian Province. Founded in 2000, Chebao has grown into a comprehensive enterprise specializing in the R&D and manufacturing of brake fluids, integrating product development, production, sales and service.

Currently, Chebao consists of three subsidiaries. We specialize in the R&D, production and sales of mid-to-high-end brake fluids. We own multiple brands including "Changan Chebao", "Jingchi" and "Junke", and provide OEM brake fluid services for hundreds of domestic brands.

Digital Transformation

SMART DATA MANAGEMENT

Fully integrated digital systems for real-time monitoring, auto-reporting, and strict quality assurance.

Automated Data Export & CoA Generation

Say goodbye to manual data entry errors. Our laboratory system seamlessly integrates with testing instruments to automate workflow processes.

  • One-click generation of customized Certificate of Analysis (CoA) reports.
  • Supports multi-dimensional table management for strict documentation formatting.
  • Ensures 100% data integrity and traceability for every DOT3, DOT4, and DOT5.1 batch.

Real-time Trend Analysis (SPC)

We rely on data, not just final inspections. By continuously monitoring the fluctuation trends of key indicators, we identify potential deviations before they happen.

  • Dynamic control charts for Equilibrium Reflux Boiling Point (ERBP) and Kinematic Viscosity.
  • Visualized dashboard tracking pH value stability across recent production batches.
  • Statistical Process Control (SPC) integration to maintain perfect process capability (Cpk).

Instant NG Alert & Group Push

Quality issues require zero-delay responses. Our digital management system features an automated alert mechanism linked directly to our internal communication groups.

  • Automated bot pushes instant alerts to the quality management group chat upon detecting non-conforming (NG) data.
  • Interactive message cards: Managers click the alert to instantly view specific out-of-spec indicators (e.g., Moisture content high).
  • Drives an immediate containment and corrective action response, ensuring no defective product leaves the lab.
